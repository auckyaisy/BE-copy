Applied Rules
  1. Grouped every 3 hours (6 data points, each 30 minutes apart).
  2. Ignore the "Running" status when determining the dominant status.
  3. If there are 3 or more non-"Running" statuses within the 3-hour group, proceed with the prediction:
    • If one status appears most frequently, select it as the dominant status.
    • If there's a tie, choose the one most frequent throughout the day (only among tied statuses).
  4. If there are fewer than 3 non-"Running" statuses, classify the entire group as "Running".
  5. When Amps and Freq are 0, and the slopes of IP, DP, IT, MT, and V are 0 within a tolerance, it is identified as EDP.
